About this chart
  • This tool uses the paper’s week-specific centile tables for six pre-pregnancy BMI groups.
  • It shows expected absolute weight in kilograms, with weight gain and approximate percentile available on hover for user measurements.
  • Built based on the data from Santos et al. Gestational weight gain charts for different body mass index groups for women in Europe, North America, and Oceania. BMC Medicine. 2018;16:201. doi:10.1186/s12916-018-1189-1.
